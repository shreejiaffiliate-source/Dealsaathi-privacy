Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Welcome to DealSaathi. DealSaathi is a marketplace platform where users can buy and sell products safely within their local community. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Photo
  • Google Account Information (if you sign in with Google)
  • Device Information
  • Location Information (only when permission is granted)
  • Product Listings
  • Listing Images
  • Chat Messages
  • Notification Token (Firebase)

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Allow you to buy and sell products.
  • Display your listings to other users.
  • Provide chat between buyers and sellers.
  • Send notifications about messages and listing updates.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Prevent fraud, spam and misuse.

Location Permission

DealSaathi requests location access only to help users discover nearby products and provide a better marketplace experience. Your location is never sold to third parties.

Camera & Photos

The Camera and Photo Library permissions are used only for uploading product images and updating your profile picture. We never access your media without your permission.

Google Sign-In

If you choose Google Sign-In, we may receive your name, email address and profile picture from your Google account. This information is used only for authentication.

Notifications

DealSaathi uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send notifications such as:

  • New Chat Messages
  • Listing Updates
  • Important Account Notifications

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information is shared only:

  • When required by law.
  • To protect users against fraud or abuse.
  • To provide app functionality.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Google Play Services

Your Rights

You may:
  • Update your profile information.
  • Delete your listings.
  • Delete your account.
  • Contact us regarding your personal information.

Children's Privacy

DealSaathi is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
