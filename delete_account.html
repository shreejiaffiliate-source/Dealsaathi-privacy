Delete Your DealSaathi Account

At DealSaathi, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal information. If you no longer wish to use DealSaathi, you can permanently request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the DealSaathi app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account (if available).
  3. Or send us an email requesting account deletion.
Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Account Information
  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Photo
  • Your Product Listings
  • Uploaded Product Images
  • Favorites / Saved Listings
  • Chat Messages
  • Notification Tokens
  • Personal Preferences

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security investigations
  • Dispute resolution

Any retained information will be securely deleted after the legally required retention period.

Processing Time

Your account deletion request is generally processed within 7 Business Days. Once completed, your personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.
